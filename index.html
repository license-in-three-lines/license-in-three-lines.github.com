The short version

Ever wished for a sweet ‘little’ permissive license? Then this is for you.

The less short version

The LITL is born out of the need to have a ‘as simple as possible’ license for when you just want to release your work to be used in any type of other work. Without the need for too much ‘lawspeak’, which could lead to reluctance to read the whole license, or lack of understanding thereof. But still making sure you maintain copyright and are not liable to provide warranty of any kind.
